About

Who Am I?

Facebook | LinkedIn | Twitter | Instagram

#research As head of R&D at ROBUR Automation I am connecting smart people to work on innovations for the factory of the future. If you are interested in industrial process optimization, smart worker assistance or virtual means of knowledge transfer and collaboration, then lets get connected on LinkedIn.

#teaching As a part time lecturer at TH Aschaffenburg I am teaching computer graphics, data visualization and game design. You can find tweets on latest news and technologies about these topics on my Twitter channel. Feel free to follow.

#creativity For a long time I have been writing textbooks and fiction alike. Some years ago I also discovered my passion for computer art and photography. In addition I do very bad sketches that I sometimes use for quizzes for my German speaking friends and fellows. Some of my more presentable work can be found on Instagram.
